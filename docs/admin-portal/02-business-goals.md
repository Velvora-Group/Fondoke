## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- **GMV (Gross Merchandise Value)**: Total booking value processed
- **Trading Volume**: Number and value of successful trades
- **User Retention**: 60% monthly active user retention
- **Contract Coverage**: Number of hotel rooms under direct contracts
- **Platform Revenue**: Commission from bookings (10-15%) + trading fees (5%) + contract margins
- **Customer Satisfaction**: NPS score > 50
