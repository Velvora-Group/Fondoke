## Vision

Fondoke aims to revolutionize the hotel booking industry by creating a comprehensive platform that combines traditional booking with a unique trading marketplace, enabling users to buy, sell, and trade hotel reservations while leveraging AI-powered search and bulk contract management.

## Primary Objectives

1. **Revenue Generation**: Create multiple revenue streams through booking commissions, trading marketplace fees, and B2B contract management
2. **Market Differentiation**: Establish Fondoke as the first platform to combine hotel booking with a peer-to-peer trading marketplace
3. **User Acquisition**: Attract 100,000+ active users in the first year through unique value proposition
4. **B2B Growth**: Secure contracts with 500+ hotels for bulk inventory management
5. **Platform Efficiency**: Reduce booking cancellation waste by enabling users to resell unwanted reservations
